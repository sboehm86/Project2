Arena

With this chart, you can observe the weakness and/or strength of one Pokémon power compared to another when using one of the 18 shown. If you click on one of the power points, certain lines will light up linking to other powers that the selected is commonly battled against. The three colors of the lines have significance:

  • If the path is YELLOW, the Pokémon with the selected power receives double the damage
  • If the path is BLUE, the Pokémon with the selected power takes half the damage
  • If the path is PURPLE, Pokémon with the the selected power is totally immune
